O que é Transparência em ES?

Podemos tentar resumir Transparência como um atributo da comunicação que tenta possibilitar que os stakeholders respondam questões a respeito do projeto de software através da divulgação de informações.

Como a literatura conceitua a transparência?

The main studies chosen by Paulinus, Bassey and Francis in their systematic literature review [4] define transparency as a non-functional requirement. However, some studies argue that transparency is actually an activity of requirements engineering since requirements are the first step in software development. Transparency has been conceptualized in terms of information disclosure or process disclosure [4.1]. From the point of view of software, information transparency means making the information the software deals with transparent [4.2]. Process transparency, on the other hand, means the software can reveal how it works, what it does, and how it does it [4.2]. Other studies define transparency as follows: Transparency can be defined as the open flow of information[1.2] and the release of information by institutions that is relevant to evaluating these institutions [1.3].
